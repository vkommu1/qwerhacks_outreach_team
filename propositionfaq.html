What is a proposition?

A proposition is proposed legislation that begins as a

referendumdirect vote in which voters can vote to either accept or reject a proposal, law, or constitutional amendment

or as an

initiative.a process in which voters can vote to placed proposed laws or amendments on the ballot through a petition for direct public voting

It becomes a proposition once it receives enough signatories to be eligible for the ballot.

Citizens can then vote "yes" or "no" to each proposition.

If it passes, it becomes part of the state constitution or statute.
